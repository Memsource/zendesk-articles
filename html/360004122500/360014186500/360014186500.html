Administrators and Project Managers with unlimited user rights can customize types of Language Quality Assessment warnings applied in projects.

To change LQA Settings, follow these steps:

  1. From the Setup  page, scroll down to the Project Settings section and click on LQA Setttings.

    The LQA Settings page opens.

  2. Select required categories and subcategories.

  3. Apply severity ratings.

  4. Click Save.

    Settings will be applied to workflow steps in which LQA is enabled.
