Some user are not able to open translated documents (Completed or translation preview) in Word 2007.
This is due the compatibility issues of Microsoft Word 2007 (know for its bugs and compatibility problems).

The best solution would be to upgrade the 10 years old software to newer version.
The other option is to download and install free Libre Office, open the docx downloaded from Memsource and save it as doc. This file then will work in Word 2007.

Libre Office can be downloaded here.
