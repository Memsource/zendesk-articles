When you first log in as a Linguist user, you will immediately be taken to the jobs dashboard, which will look something like this:

 

The dashboard provides an overview of all the jobs you are currently assigned to and the status of each. In addition to the New, Accepted, and Completed statuses which are used in Memsource, the Mobile App also includes Overdue Jobs, Due in 24 hrs, and Cancelled Jobs, making it easier for you to prioritize and plan your work.

If you select any of these sections, you will be taken to a list of the relevant jobs. You can search this list using the search bar, or narrow the search further by using the filter options.

Note: When you log in as a Project Manager, you will be taken to a Project Dashboard.

Reviewing Jobs

If you’ve received a notification saying that a job has been assigned to you, it’s important to respond as soon as possible. With the Memsource Mobile App, you can review all the key details about a job and even view the original source file even, if you don’t have your computer with you.

The key information about a job can be seen in the Jobs list. If you want to find out more, select the job. To see the original source file, press the three dots (...) in the top right-hand corner. From this Job detail page, you can also change the status of jobs (see the next bit below).

Note: With an iOS device, you can preview the file from the app. With an Android device, you need to download and open the file.

Accepting, Declining, and Marking Jobs as Complete

Once you’ve reviewed the source file, you can choose to either accept or decline the job. If you accept the job, you can then edit the job in the Memsource Mobile app. Once the job is complete, you can also set the job to Completed.
