The Linguist role is that of a translator. Jobs are assigned to Linguists by Project Managers and when notification is received, the Linguist logs into the Linguist Portal to accept and complete assignments.

The role was designed for freelance translators from outside a translation company and they do not have any project management rights.

Set Linguist User Rights

Linguist user rights are generally about ability to use and modify Translation Resources. The Net rate scheme and Price list are used to help calculate Quotes. Rights are set by an Administrator or Project Manager.

To set Linguist user rights, follow these steps:

  1. From the Users page, select a user and click Edit.

    The Edit User page opens.

  2. Ensure the Role is Linguist.

    Settings specific to the Linguist role and Memsource edition are displayed.

  3. Select required restrictions and click Save.

    Settings are applied to the specified user.
