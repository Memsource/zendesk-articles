New Features and Improvements

Magento 2 Connector Now Available

Our Magento 2 connector is now available. This connector allows you to translate your Magento products, categories, pages, and blocks either by manually or automatically importing them.

 

Syncing Languages in Zendesk

You can now change the languages in your Zendesk account and have those changes appear in your Memsource connector for Zendesk. 

 

Memsource Translate

The beta version of Memsource Translate is now available. Memsource Translate (Beta) evaluates the quality of different machine translation engines, automatically compare them, and find the best fit for your project. This will help make working with machine translation engines easier and more effective.
