There are two ways how to submit a request to Memsource Support team.

  1. Using the “Contact us” button in Memsource web / Linguist Portal; this will open the form in a new window on the Memsource Help Center.
  2. Using the "Submit a Request" button Memsource Help Center; when you click the “Submit a Request” button in the Help Center you will be asked to log in to your Memsource account. Use your Memsource username or Single-sign-on (if applicable to your account). If you have more than one Memsource account, please use the one relevant to your request.

As of 1 September 2019, the support email address is no longer active. It won’t be possible to contact the Technical Support team via this email address.

Will this change the type of support I get?

Don’t worry—you’ll still have access to our Technical Support team. We’re only making this change so you can receive the best and most efficient support possible. When a request is made through the Help Form or the Help Widget, it automatically provides us with much of the information we need about the issue you are facing.

This means that we are less likely to require additional information to address it. We will be able to start working to find solutions faster, providing you with a better service and saving you time and effort.
