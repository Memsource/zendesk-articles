Contentful lets you create, manage, and distribute your websites, images, or documents across platforms.

Using Memsource, you will be able to translate your content by either manually uploading it or using  Automated Project Creation to initiate jobs based on rules that you define in a Project Template. This article provides a step-by-step guide to setting up and using the Memsource extension for Contentful.

Set up the Connector

Note: You will need to have pop-ups enabled in your browser in order to connect.

Before setting up the connector in Memsource, you'll want to log into your Contentful account. Being logged into Contentful will allow Memsource to get your account information during the setup process.

Navigate to Memsource and log in. Go to the main Setup and scroll down to the Integrations section. Click on Connectors. After clicking on New, you can choose the connector you wish to create.  If you're using entry-level localization in your Contentful instance, please select our Contentful (Entry-level) connector. For field-level localization, please use the Contentful connector.  Then, click Connect to Contentful.

After you click Connect to Contentful, a pop-up window will appear that should show that your authentication was successful. Close the window, and you'll be brought back to your Memsource account. If you’ve done everything correctly, you should see a little checkmark next to the Connect to Contentful button. After this, click Save.
