Users can have more than one Memsource account as long as each account has a unique username. Translators can also work for several different agencies as their Linguists and can have their own Team Start edition account as well.


However, it is important to always use the relevant username and password, since only the correct username can access the specific jobs assigned under that username.


Sometimes, the best solution is to a single Team Start account and act like a Vendor. In this situation, you would simply ask the agency to share the projects or jobs.


Please note that you cannot use your Linguist account for translating jobs other than those assigned to you by a Project Manager.
