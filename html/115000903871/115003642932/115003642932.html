Memsource includes a Find and Replace window. In order to access this feature, press Ctrl+F. The Find and Replace window will then be displayed at the bottom of the Editor. This allows you to:

  • Switch between the source and target
  • Skip to the next or previous occurrence of the searched word
  • Match the case
  • Toggle the Replace option On/Off by clicking on the black triangle (on the right side)
  • Replace one by one or Replace All (in the target)

 

Note: Replacing words in the source can be done only when in Edit Source mode and only segment by segment.
