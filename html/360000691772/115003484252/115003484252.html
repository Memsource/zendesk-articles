Memsource includes an integral Quality Assurance module, but users in need of a more detailed or customizable Quality Assurance check can utilize the integration by ApSIC Xbench.

Not only can the files be exported from Memsource and opened in the Xbench, ApSIC has also developed a connector to provide instant access by using our APIs. Memsource APIs are currently available for all paid Memsource editions including the Linguist or Vendor users of such editions. However, daily limitations need to be taken into account.
