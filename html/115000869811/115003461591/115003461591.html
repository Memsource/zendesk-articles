Memsource Personal Edition is a free of charge edition of Memsource intended for occasional translators.
 
  • Limitations of Personal Edition
Only 2 jobs (files for translation) can be present in the Personal account at any given time. The maximum file size of each of these is set to 10 MB.
 
  • Support for Personal Edition users
Personal edition users can only use the community support available from our Support Center.
 
  • Translation Workbenches
Both of the Memsource Editors (Web and Desktop) are available in this edition.
 
  • Memsource APIs
Memsource APIs are only available for paid editions, therefore they are not provided for Personal Edition users.
  • Upgrading and Downgrading your edition
If the Personal Edition is no longer sufficient for your needs, you can always consider upgrading to a 1+Freelancer or even Team Start Edition. This can easily be done in your account in the Setup - Subscription - Add user or upgrade edition. 
If you wish to downgrade back to the Personal Edition, please contact Memsource Support that can downgrade your edition for you (please make sure you comply with the above mentioned limitations since additional data exceeding beyond the limitation will be deleted).
