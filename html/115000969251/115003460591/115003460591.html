Is Memsource down? Are you experiencing intermittent connection, or do you feel that Memsource does not respond as quickly as it usually does?

Any critical problems with Memsource service are listed on Memsource Status Page, where you can see the status of our servers and services.

It is possible to subscribe to email or SMS messages so you will be informed when new incidents happen and when they are resolved. Alternatively, it is also possible to subscribe to only the current incident and be informed when it is resolved.
