Kentico is a cloud-based content management system that allows teams to easily collaborate on creating websites, online stores, and community sites. Memsource supports versions 11 and 12 of Kentico. Below, you'll find instructions on how to connect your Kentico account to Memsource.

Set up the Connector in Kentico

In order to establish a connection between Memsource and Kentico, you will need to enable both REST Translation Service and REST Translation in your Kentico account.

To enable REST Translation Service, click on Settings. In the left-hand column, open the Content menu and select Translation services. In the General section, select Enable REST translation service.

Next, you'll need to enable REST Translation. To do this, you'll need to have Global administrator privileges enabled in your Kentico account. Got to Settings and in the left-hand column, open the Integration menu. Select REST and at the top of the page, click on Service enabled. Then, click Save.

 You will also need to acquire the Kentico URL, which you can obtain from the Sites section in Kentico. Copy this and save it—you'll need it later in Memsource. Once you have all of this information from Kentico, open Memsource.

Set up the Connector in Memsource

Navigate to Memsource and log in. Go to Setup and scroll down to the Integrations section. Click on Connectors. After clicking on New, you can choose the connector you wish to create. Give it a name and change the Type to Kentico.

Enter your Kentico URL, Kentico User name, and your Kentico password. Then click Test connection. Once you’ve done this, you should see a small check mark next to the Test Connection button. Click on Save.
